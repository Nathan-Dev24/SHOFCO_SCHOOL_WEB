School Life

Students at SHOFCO Community thrive on consistency and schedules, therefore, each day follows a fairly regular schedule.

The students' day typically begins at 8:00 am and concludes at 3:45 pm, with the exception of Wednesday when we dismiss at 1:00 pm for teacher development.

The morning begins with morning announcements, after which students have time for movement. Students will engage in variety of activities, including wall pushups, jumping jacks or other physical activities, to get them ready to focus for the day's learning.
